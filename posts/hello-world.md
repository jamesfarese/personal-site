# hello, world.

this is the first post. i've been meaning to write for years, and i keep not doing it. so here we are.

the blank page is the hardest part. not because there's nothing to say, but because there's too much. every sentence wants to be the right one. every paragraph wants to earn its place.

so i'm lowering the bar. this is just a hello. a signal that the antenna is on.

---

i build companies. that's the short version. the longer version involves a lot of whiteboards, late nights, cold coffee, and the particular feeling of watching something you made start to actually work.

there's a moment — and you only get it if you've built something from nothing — where the thing you imagined becomes real. where a user does exactly what you hoped they'd do and you think: *oh. it works.*

that feeling is what i'm chasing. everything else is infrastructure.

---

i'll write here about building, thinking, and the weird overlap between art and commerce. about the companies i've made and the ones i'm still making. about what i've learned and what i'm still figuring out.

* losing is learning. learning is winning.

see you next time.
